ALARM CLOCK

EVOLUTION OF ALARM CLOCK

  • User-settable mechanical alarm clocks date back at least to 15th-century Europe.
  • These early alarm clocks had a ring of holes in the clock dial and were set by placing a pin in the appropriate hole.
  • The word "alarm" means literally “to arms” or “to your weapons.”

FACTS ABOUT ALARM CLOCK

  • 83% of 19-29-year-olds use a phone alarm.
  • About 10% of people ignore their alarm the first three times.
  • The average time an alarm clock is set for is 7:04 am.
